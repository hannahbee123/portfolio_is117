After graduating from East Brunswick High School in 2009, I pursued a career in the beauty industry as a hairstylist, although I always knew I would return to school. Being in the hair industry gave me the opportunity to meet people from all walks of life -- this turned out to be a critical factor in how I decided what I wanted to study in school. I decided to transfer to NJIT to study Human Computer Interaction because I am interested in understanding how we interact with technology and how to optimize it to serve our ever-changing needs. Spending years behind the chair at salons taught me great communication skills and I hope to apply those skills with the knowledge I acquire from my time in college to explore the symbiotic relationship between humans and computers.
